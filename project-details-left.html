Tourism Activities in Lango

Explore the vibrant activities that define the Lango Sub-Region, from cultural tours to fishing and political tourism.

Unveil Lango’s Vibrant Adventures

Dive into the heart of Lango’s 15,570.7 km² homeland, where 2.5 million souls weave a tapestry of culture, history, and nature. From sacred dances to serene lakes, discover activities that ignite the spirit.

Cultural Tours

Immerse in Lango’s 149 clans, witness Okeme and Abuda dances, and explore villages steeped in heritage.

Fishing on Lake Kwania

Join fishermen on Lake Kwania, mastering traditional techniques amid serene waters.

Political Tourism

Visit Milton Obote’s ancestral home and uncover Lango’s pivotal role in Uganda’s history.

Hiking Got Ngeta

Conquer Got Ngeta Rock for breathtaking vistas and join the annual Mini Run challenge.

Farming Experiences

Work alongside Lango farmers, sowing seeds and harvesting crops in fertile fields.

Festivals & Events

Celebrate with Lango’s clans at vibrant festivals, filled with music, dance, and community spirit.

Plan Your Lango Adventure

The Lango Sub-Region, home to 2.57 million people, celebrates a vibrant heritage, stunning landscapes, and a thriving community in mid-northern Uganda.
