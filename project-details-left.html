Discover Exciting Activities in Lango

The Lango Sub-Region offers a diverse range of activities that showcase its rich cultural heritage, vibrant economy, and historical significance. From immersive cultural tours and traditional festivals to economic pursuits like fishing and farming, and political tourism exploring sites linked to figures like Milton Obote, Lango invites visitors to engage with its dynamic way of life.

Highlights of Lango Activities

Cultural Tours

Visit traditional villages, witness Okeme and Abuda dances, and engage with Lango clans during immersive cultural tours.

Fishing on Lake Kwania

Join local fishermen on Lake Kwania or Kyoga to experience traditional fishing techniques and enjoy fresh catches.

Political Tourism

Explore sites like Milton Obote’s ancestral home and learn about Lango’s role in Uganda’s political history.

Hiking Got Ngetta

Hike Got Ngetta Rock for stunning views and participate in the annual Mini Run and Rock Challenge.
