Your Gateway to Lango’s Heart

Explore the soul of Lango, a 15,570.7 km² homeland where 2.5 million people weave a vibrant tapestry of culture, nature, and history. From sacred sites to thrilling adventures, let our guide lead you.

Welcome to Lango

Nestled in Northern Uganda, Lango’s 149 clans, led by the *Won Nyaci*, have thrived since their 16th-century migration from Abyssinia. Discover a land of lakes, hills, and stories, where heroes like John Akii Bua inspire pride.

Cultural Tours

Join vibrant Okeme and Abuda dances, visit traditional villages, and connect with Lango’s clan heritage.

Fishing on Lake Kwania

Cast nets with local fishermen on Lake Kwania, savoring fresh catches amid serene waters.

Hiking Got Ngeta

Trek Got Ngeta Rock for stunning views and join the annual Mini Run challenge.

Otuke Hills

Explore the birthplace of Lango, where Ongora Okubal led clans in the 16th century, steeped in resilience.

Barlonyo Memorial

Reflect at Barlonyo, a Lira District site honoring the 2004 healing of Lango’s spirit.

Practical Tips

Stay in Lira’s cozy guesthouses, savor local dishes like malakwang, and book with trusted tour operators like Lango Heritage Tours for a seamless visit.
