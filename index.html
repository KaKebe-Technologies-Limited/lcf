About Lango

Discover the Soul of Lango’s Vibrant Heritage

Nestled in mid-northern Uganda, Lango Sub-Region is a cultural mosaic of 149 clans, ancient traditions, and breathtaking landscapes. From the rhythmic Okeme dances to the serene shores of Lake Kwania, Lango invites you to explore its rich history and dynamic community.

  • Home to iconic sites like Got Ngetta Rock.
  • Vibrant cultural festivals and music.
  • Growing hub for education and ICT innovation.
